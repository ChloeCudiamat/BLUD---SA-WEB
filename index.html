The Crade of Noble Heroes

or Bulacan - the place so full of prideful people (I mean, no one could really blame them), the best chicharon ever, and ancient enough churches. So full of history and chaos, all in a Bulakenyo way.

The Surfing Capital

or La Union- the place full of ripped people that live by the sea, very sunkissed, and will drag you to surf.In LU, nobody’s in a rush—except maybe the waves.

The Land of Salt

or Pangasinan - millions of beaches and a lot of salty (joke!) people. Bangus too good, not too salty for someplace with a lot of salt? Island-hopping is a must here.
